In 1936 General Motors created several front companies for the purpose of purchasing and dismantling the trolley car system.

The decline of the tram system in North America could be attributed to many things labor strikes, the Great Depression, regulations that were unfavorable to operators but perhaps the primary cause was having a group of powerful men from rival sectors of the auto industry working together to ensure its destruction.
